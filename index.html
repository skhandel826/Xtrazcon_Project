Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I register with HirePoint?

Registration is simple! You can sign up through our website by providing your basic information. Once registered, you can browse job listings, apply to positions, and access our career resources. Our team will also reach out to provide personalized guidance based on your profile.

Is there any registration fee?

No, registration with HirePoint is completely free for job seekers. We believe in making quality recruitment services accessible to everyone. Our services are funded through partnerships with leading companies.

How can I prepare for interviews?

HirePoint provides comprehensive interview preparation support including mock interviews, technical assessments, and one-on-one coaching sessions. Our expert mentors provide personalized guidance based on the specific role you're applying for. Access to interview preparation materials is available in your candidate dashboard.

What is the success rate of placements?

HirePoint has a 95%+ success rate for candidates who actively engage with our programs and follow our guidance. Since our inception in 2020, we've successfully placed 500+ professionals in leading companies. The success depends on active participation in training and mock interviews.

Can I apply to multiple jobs at once?

Yes, absolutely! We encourage you to apply to multiple positions that match your skills and interests. However, make sure your resume and profile are customized for each application. Our team recommends applying to 5-10 relevant positions to increase your chances of getting interviews.

How long does the placement process take?

The placement timeline varies depending on your qualifications, the job role, and company requirements. On average, the process takes 2-6 weeks from application to final offer. Some positions may move faster, while others may take longer based on the recruitment process of the hiring company.

What qualifications do I need?

We work with candidates at all levels - freshers with degrees, experienced professionals, and career changers. Different companies have different requirements, but we have opportunities across skill levels. Our team will match you with positions suitable for your qualifications and experience.

Do you provide resume building help?

Yes, resume optimization is one of our core services. Our HR experts review your resume and provide detailed feedback to make it stand out to recruiters. We help you highlight your achievements, skills, and experience in a way that catches the attention of hiring managers.

Can I work with HirePoint while employed?

Absolutely! Many of our successful placements come from candidates who were employed while exploring new opportunities. We maintain complete confidentiality and can schedule coaching sessions and interviews at times convenient for you.

What if I don't get selected in the first attempt?

Don't worry! We provide feedback after each interview attempt and work with you to improve. Our mentors identify areas for improvement and provide targeted coaching. Most candidates succeed on their second or third attempt with proper guidance from our experts.
