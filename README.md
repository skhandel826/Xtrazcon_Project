A modern, responsive HR & recruitment agency website featuring job listings, interactive chatbot, company partner carousel, and industry insights. Built with HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript.

Features:
Job listings with filtering (Fresher/Experienced)
12+ company partner carousel
AI chatbot widget
Industry insights section
Contact form
Fully responsive design
Smooth animations & glassmorphism effect
