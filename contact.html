Frequently Asked Contact Questions

What is the best time to contact you?

We are available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM IST. For urgent matters, you can reach us on WhatsApp anytime. We usually respond within 24 hours.

How quickly will I get a response?

Email inquiries are typically responded to within 24 hours. For urgent matters, calling us directly or messaging on WhatsApp will get you a faster response.

Can I visit your office?

Yes! We welcome visits by appointment. Please call ahead at +91 9876543210 or email info@hirepoint.com to schedule a meeting with our team.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Absolutely! We offer free virtual consultations via video call for all job seekers. Schedule a consultation by contacting us through any of the methods listed above.

What should I include in my inquiry?

Please provide your name, email, phone number, and a clear description of your inquiry. If it's regarding a specific job, please mention the job title and company name.

Is there a cost for contacting support?

No, all our contact and consultation services are completely free for job seekers. There are no hidden charges or fees for reaching out to our team.
